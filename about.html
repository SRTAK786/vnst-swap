About V Network Services

V Network Services is a crypto company aiming to connect every individual with blockchain technology through its unique token. The company is single-owner operated and provides users with staking options for the token.

We offer every user a minimum guaranteed profit of 52%, and up to 100% or unlimited profit, provided they stay long-term with the company and its projects. Our token supply is fully public — no team member or the company holds any private tokens.

Our founder started as a networker in the crypto world, working with multiple companies globally. Observing that many users suffered losses, he decided to create a project where every member benefits fairly.

Our mission is to help the poor by providing technology access to all, changing the mindset of people who misunderstand online markets, and enabling everyone to benefit from the crypto space.
